IT Department

Department

Information Technology:

Teaching the basics of information technology, pattern and letter recognition, software, digital signal processing, computer vision, multimedia, information architecture, and information networks. Computer systems, applications, and technologies are involved in all aspects of life. Therefore, the need for professionals trained in computer applications is necessary to keep pace with the information revolution and meet the requirements of the labor market. The IT Department consists of different curriculum packages to cover different computer applications and also contains programming and research application packages aimed at solving real local market problems. This department enables students to acquire the necessary knowledge and skills to be able to analyze, develop, and program IT systems in any governmental or private organization and authorities.




This is an explanatory video for the IT department


Program Educational Objectives (PEOs):

  1. Enhance problem-solving ability based on creative thinking and basic engineering knowledge.
  2. Enhancing the ability to design and analyze ICT.
  3. Enhancing field professional skills and the ability to acquire new technology.
  4. Improve teamwork and communication ability.
  5. Enhancing global capacity.

After completing the program the student can be able to:

• Enhance problem-solving ability, creative thinking and basic engineering knowledge.
• Use experiments and practices to gain more experience in solving problems.
• How to make best use of data mining to improve problem solving.
• Learn more about different tools and apps (websites, blogs, competition boards).
• How to clearly design a problem-solving idea before implementing it.
• Identify strengths and weaknesses in design and analysis capacity in the field of ICT.
