Mechatronics :

Mechatronics technology system requires knowledge mainly in mechanics, electronics, control and computer. Mechatronics systems sometimes use intellectual technology because they can mimic the way humans think. Today, mechatronics is included in almost all devices, as it is not only specialized in robots or factories, but rather it helps in obtaining a better product, as is the case in the electronic fuel injection system, ABS car brakes, household machines such as automatic washing machines, or even in children’s toys.




This is an explanatory video for theMechatronics department


Mechatronics technology applications:

  1. Use of PLC programmable logic controller in industrial systems.
  2. Supervisory data acquisition (SCADA) systems.
  3. Building Management Systems (BMS).
  4. Computer hardware such as CD-ROM mechanism design.
  5. Remote control systems.
