Department

Autotronics:

Autotronics is an engineering specialty concerned with the field of engineering modern cars and their electronics. It is considered one of the specializations of mechanical engineering, but it specializes in the mechanics of modern cars and their electricity. It is concerned with the technological aspect of cars directly. It was introduced as a result of the local and scientific market need, in order to meet the market’s need mainly for Engineers specialized in advanced automotive technology.




This is an explanatory video for the Autotronics department


General objectives of the Autotronics program:

  1. Providing graduates with the practical skills, information and knowledge necessary to practice work in the autotronics profession, which enables them to advance in practical life and compete in the labor market.
  2. Providing graduates with personal and transferable skills, management skills, and work practice in the autotronics profession. These skills include communication and the ability to scientific analysis, description of problems, and work to solve them.
  3. Effective contribution to the implementation of the desired national policies to meet the professional needs of society by providing it with graduates with high professional skills in the field of autoronics.
  4. Encouraging the establishment of small service projects in the field of microelectronic applications in cars to participate in the labor market with the graduate relying on himself.
