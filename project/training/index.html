Cooperation protocols :

Beni Suef Technological University strives to conclude many cooperation protocols with various bodies and institutions to enhance ways of joint cooperation and advance technical and technological education, in addition to developing the university’s scientific and cultural relations with all institutions in order to enhance scientific cooperation and exchange of experiences, and to ensure the achievement of the desired goals in order to graduate A generation capable of competing in the local, regional and international labor market, and providing the necessary experiences for university students at the scientific and practical levels.



Some of Cooperation protocols :

• cooperation protocol with the Higher Technological Institute in Beni Suef.
• cooperation protocol with the Egyptian Workers’ Association.
• cooperation protocol with Nahda University.
• cooperation protocol with the Military Technological College.
• cooperation protocol with Assiut University.
• cooperation protocol with the Nuclear Materials Authority.
• cooperation protocol with the Egyptian Electricity Holding Company.
• cooperation protocol with the Arab Organization for Industrialization.
• cooperation protocol between Beni Suef University and El Sewedy Electric Foundation.
• cooperation agreement with Samsung.
•cooperation protocol with the Egyptian Space Agency.
