Department

Prosthetics and Orthotics Technology:

The program provides advanced educational and technological services at a global level of quality that meet and keep pace with the needs of the labor market and society in the field of orthotics and prosthetics. The program also meets the needs of the labor market for competent technologists in the field of orthotics and prosthetics technology to achieve a competitive advantage for the technologists who graduate the program within the framework of high professional ethics.




This is an explanatory video for the Prosthetics and Orthotics Technology department


Graduates' fields of work:

  1. Hospitals
  2. Factories and companies that produce prosthetic limbs
  3. The sales department in companies that specialize in selling and supplying prosthetic limbs
  4. The field of quality control in prosthetics and orthotics.
  5. Rehabilitation centres
