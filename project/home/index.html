Information About BTU

The presidential decree establishing Beni Suef Technological University was issued in the Official Gazette on June 3, 2019, regarding the issuance of Law No. 72 of 2019 establishing technological universities. This step is considered an important shift in creating a new path for technical education in Egypt. Technological universities are an extension of the path of technical education students, which work to provide them with practical and scientific skills to keep pace with the requirements of the local and international labor market, through the technological programs that are implemented in the colleges affiliated with the university, and which were developed based on the needs of national projects and the geography of the universities.



International cooperation between the university and South Korea:

            • An agreement signed between the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research and the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA).
            • Obtaining accreditation for the university’s programs, which include the Mechatronics Program and the Information Technology (ICT) Program, 
              for a period of 6 years from 2022 to 2028.
            • The presence of a technical support team from the Korea University of Technology and Education (Korea Tech), and the ABEEK Foundation, 
              which is responsible for ensuring quality and accreditation of technological and engineering programs in South Korea.



Based on the approval of the University Council, Beni Suef Technological University awards the following academic degrees:

            • Professional Higher Diploma in Technology in the specialty.
            • Professional bachelor’s degree in technology in the specialty.
            • Professional master’s degree in technology in the specialty.
            • Professional doctorate in technology in the specialty.
